Care that starts in the classroom and grows into the community.

We place one dedicated nurse and one doctor inside partner schools, building a foundation of preventive care that we're expanding outward to families and the wider community.

Our Path

One school at a time, then beyond the school gate.

School

Embedded care

A resident nurse and visiting doctor handle daily checkups, first response, and health education on campus.

Community

Family outreach

Health camps and parent workshops extend the same care to siblings and households around each school.

Society

Public health network

Partner schools become local health hubs, linking neighborhoods to ongoing care and referral support.

What We Provide

Two people. Every school day.

N

Resident Nurse

On-site daily for checkups, first aid, medication tracking, and health record-keeping for every student.

D

Visiting Doctor

Scheduled clinical visits for diagnosis, referrals, vaccination drives, and consultation with families.

+

Health Education

Age-appropriate sessions on hygiene, nutrition, and mental wellbeing built into the school calendar.
